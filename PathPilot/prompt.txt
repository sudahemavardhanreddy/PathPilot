Prompt:

Build a web-based platform named “PathPilot” – a One-Stop Personalized Career & Education Advisor for students after Class 10 and 12.

Objective:
To provide structured, reliable, and personalized academic and career guidance that helps students select appropriate streams, degree programs, and career paths while increasing awareness about government degree colleges.

Core Features:

Student Profile System

Registration and login.

Academic background entry (10th/12th details).

Interest and career preference selection.

Aptitude & Interest Assessment

MCQ-based test module.

Categorization into domains (Analytical, Creative, Technical, Social, etc.).

Automated stream recommendation (Arts/Science/Commerce).

Course-to-Career Pathway Mapping

Visual representation:
Stream → Degree → Job Roles → Higher Studies → Competitive Exams.

Interactive and easy-to-understand pathway diagrams.

Government College Directory

Filter by location.

Display nearby government colleges.

Show available degree programs and eligibility.

Degree Comparison Dashboard

Compare courses based on duration, scope, career outcomes, and growth potential.

AI-Based Recommendation Engine

Match student assessment scores with suitable streams and degrees.

Provide reasoning for each recommendation.

User Experience

Clean, modern, mobile-friendly interface.

Simple navigation for first-time users.

Tech Stack Suggestion:
Frontend: React
Backend: Node.js / Django
Database: MySQL / MongoDB
Basic AI scoring logic for recommendations.

Goal:
Develop a scalable prototype that demonstrates how structured guidance can reduce confusion, improve informed decision-making, and positively influence higher education enrollment.
